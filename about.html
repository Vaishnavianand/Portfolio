A developer and aspiring designer working towards creating a sustainable and socially responsible future.
“Differences of habit and language are nothing at all if our aims are identical and our hearts are open.” - Albus Dumbledore
I am a web developer based in Mumbai with a diverse skillset across UI development, design, research and testing. Starting my professional journey with a bachelors of technology in Electronics and Communication from SRM, I have since worked with corporations, startups and non-profits.
Due to my father's government job, I moved to a new city in India every three years as a child. My childhood was spent stumbling into different cultures, deciphering their ways, and attempting to fit in. Through these experiences, I learned how to empathise with people from various backgrounds and how human nature is inherently the same across cultures.I bring these experiences into my design process to discover solutions which impact the environment and lives of the financially ridden.
