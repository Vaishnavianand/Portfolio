Viewpoint PMS
RCI integration with Viewpoint PMS.

** As this was a client-side project which is still in the delivering phase , I am not at the liberty to disclose the intricate details or use website images and screenshots. **

RCI the world’s premier vacation exchange company under the Panorama portfolio of leading travel membership brands, announced its integration with the global, cloud-based resort management system, Viewpoint PMS.

BACKGROUND

RCI tends to improve accessibility and promote time savings for their members by integrating with Viewpoint PMS, an automated, cloud-based resort management system. Owners can deposit their weeks for exchange via RCI via their smartphone or web browser. Other automated processes include generating inbound guest notifications, managing membership expiration dates, and updating renewals - all of which improve resort operations in a streamlined manner.

ROLE

I worked as a UI developer in a team of 3 developers and 2 UX designers from the client team at Cognizant Technology and Solutions.

TOOLS

Visual Studio Code, BitBucket, JIRA, InVision Design

CODING

HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, React JS, Node JS

THE SITUATION

The RCI team wanted to provide their resort management partners with the best affiliation management platforms not only to enhance the sales but also to give them the comfort of managing their timeshares and enhancing the long term business.
Viewpoint PMS will prove to be a powerful integration tool with revenue management,channel mamangement and certified payment processing.

UNDERSTANDING THE TASK

The main motive of the development team was to integrate the Viewpoint PMS step by step with RCI in terms of the following features keeping in mind the performance testing:
  1. Design
  2. Functionalities
  3. Content
After the completion of the above, move on to the security and deployment challenges and resolve them efficiently.

METHODOLOGY

ACTION PROCESS

What did I do ?
  • I began by gaining an understanding of the project's flow in terms of integrating the travel components and interacting with the client team to ascertain their objectives.
  • My main task was to update the UI of the 'weeks' members travel integration by changing certain form fields such as space bank keys and address inputs, as the travel integration had already begun.
  • Following the week's integration, we moved on to 'points' member integration, where I started by coding the forms in Formik for the members to sign up or login with their personal information and choose payment methods, which I integrated using 3rd party APIs.
  • After completing the coding of each component, I handed over the development testing to the QA team, with whom I worked to resolve performance issues.
  • I collaborated on BitBucket with the other developers on my team and kept the client's business team up to date with Jira stories.

RESULT AND IMPACT

  • We delivered the integrations to the client page by page after thoroughly testing each component and launched the Viewpoint PMS integrated pages for limited user testing.
  • As a result of our solutions, the client was able to achieve the goal of improving resort operations by automating processes such as managing member data, certified payments, revenue management, and so on.
